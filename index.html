The Idea

The main idea of making this project is the need to create a mobile application that football players can share their experiences and find each other.

We are talking about players, coaches, sports directors, teams and fans.

In general, the result of the project will allow the players to communicate with each other. But it depends on the user, the project can benefit you in one way or another.

For example, in the case of:

  • Players, the application will help you share your skills by uploading photos or videos and even find equipment if someday you run out of equipment or just want to change the air.
  • Coaches, the application will help you find new players to improve their teams or find a team for the case of running out.
  • Sports directors, the application will help them find players or coaches for their teams or find a team for the case of running out.
  • Teams, the application will help you find new players and coaches to reinforce their teams, sports directors to reinforce the team's management and even fans posting the hours of their teams' matches.
  • Fans, the application will help them to know the schedules to the latest news of their favorite players, coaches or teams.
